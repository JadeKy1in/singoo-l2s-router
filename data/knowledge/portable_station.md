# Portable Power Station Series

## PS5000 — 5kWh Portable Station
- **Capacity**: 5120Wh, LiFePO4, 3500+ cycles to 80%
- **Output**: 5000W pure sine wave (10000W surge), 230V/110V switchable
- **Ports**: 4x AC, 4x USB-C (100W PD), 2x USB-A, 2x DC5521, 1x car socket
- **Input**: 3000W AC fast charge (0-80% in 1.5h), 2000W solar (2x MPPT)
- **Weight**: 52kg with wheels and telescopic handle
- **Display**: LCD with real-time power flow, SOC, estimated runtime
- **Features**: UPS mode (< 15ms switch), app control via WiFi/BT, daisy-chain up to 4 units

## PS2000 — 2kWh Portable Station
- **Capacity**: 2048Wh, LiFePO4
- **Output**: 2400W (4800W surge)
- **Weight**: 22kg, compact form factor
- **Solar**: 1200W MPPT input

## Target Markets
Outdoor events, construction sites, emergency backup, camping/overlanding.
Home backup for Southeast Asian markets with frequent outages.
MOQ: 20 units (PS5000), 50 units (PS2000). Lead time: 3-6 weeks.
