# EV Charger Series

## DCFC120 — 120kW DC Fast Charger
- **Power**: 120kW, dual-gun simultaneous (60kW+60kW)
- **Connectors**: CCS2 / CHAdeMO / GB/T
- **Input**: 400V AC ±15%, 50/60Hz
- **Output**: 200-1000V DC, 300A max
- **Protocol**: OCPP 2.0.1 compliant, ISO 15118 ready
- **Protection**: IP54, over-temp, over-current, ground fault detection
- **Display**: 7" touch screen, multi-language (EN/CN/AR/ES)

## ACFC22 — 22kW AC Wallbox
- **Power**: 22kW, single/tri-phase 32A
- **Connector**: Type 2 socket with shutter
- **Features**: RFID card, app control via WiFi/BT, dynamic load balancing
- **Protection**: IP65, IK10 impact resistance
- **Certifications**: CE, TUV, ROHS

## Target Markets
Fleet operators, highway service stations, commercial parking, residential.
MOQ: 5 units (DCFC120), 20 units (ACFC22). Lead time: 3-8 weeks.
